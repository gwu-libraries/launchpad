To narrow the search results

After your search results display you may make selections from the Refine Results list which will narrow your search results. Refine options include library, content type, language, and other characteristics.

Click on a title in the search results to:

  • view more details about a title
  • access an electronic resource (eBook, Streaming Audio, Streaming Video, etc.)
  • request a title through the consortium loan service (CLS)
  • request a title through the interlibrary loan service
